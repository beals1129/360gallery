Subscription Billing Models

Steppay supports companies to configure pricing plans with multiple subscription billing models. From fixed recurring to usage-based plans, Steppay covers all complexity of subscription models. Businesses can also add different sets of options and features that suit their business.

  • One-time : one time billing products, no recurring
  • Fixed fee : charging a fixed amount on a recurring basis
  • Fixed fee + add options : charging a fixed amount on a recurring basis + add options
  • Seat-based : billing customers a flat fee for services per user or account
  • Per unit-based : Setting the same amount per unit and charge according to the number of quantity
  • Package : Selling products by its package price
  • Volume-based : More discounts are applied as you buy in bulk
  • Stair-step :charging only for what they use with the fixed price tier
  • Tiered : charges for each unit based on the price of its corresponding tier and then adding the totals for each tier

Subscription Management

This solution is a self-service feature where it allows end-users to manage their own subscription status from their own account. Merchants don't need to take care of all the CS requests regarding subscription changes of users. Features include the following lists:

  • pause subscription
  • cancel subscription
  • change of billing date
  • custom subscription products
  • change of delivery address
  • change of payment methods
